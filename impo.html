Time Management website
Description:

Time management is the coordination of tasks and activities to maximize the effectiveness of an individual's efforts.

Importance of Time management
  1. Effective time management helps leaders, entrepreneurs, and small business owners achieve their goals.
  2. Managing your time wisely improves work-life balance and increases happiness.
  3. Good time management also reduces stress and allows you to achieve your goals faster and easier.
  4. ime management is important to help you prioritise better and increase your productivity.
  5. Good time management helps you work smarter, not harder, ensuring you get more productive work done in less time.
